12 am

12 am is a social media app where you can only post from 12 AM to 1 AM. The goal of this app was to learn about user generated content. How to manage blocking users, reporting users, and having a main feed streaming. This app displays the most recent posts from 12 am to 1am accross the world. It also gets the users location and saves it to CloudKit.

Decade

Decade helps you find apparel and clothing styles based on the decade that you select. This is the best place to search for 2000s apparel, 90's style, 70's trends, and more! You can save images, search for different clothing styles, or select preset timeframes. Now you can share with your friends and comment on pictures. Take a picture and create your own decades.

CloudKit & Firebase
